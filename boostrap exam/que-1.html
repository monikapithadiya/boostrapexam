Canada is a vast country in North America that stretches from the Atlantic to the Pacific Ocean, and into the Arctic Ocean. It's the world's second largest country by land area and has the longest coastline.Canada features black-blue lakes, numerous rivers, majestic western mountains, rolling central plains, and forested eastern valleys. The Canadian Shield, a hilly region of lakes and swamps, stretches across northern Canada and has some of the oldest rocks on Earth
